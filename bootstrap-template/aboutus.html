The Snow Tree

The Snow Tree has been carefully located to offer a comfortable service of hospitality as well as locations unparalled anywhere in the world.

Mark Twain seems to have visited this Himalayan city and penned down his impression about Darjeeling as the 'land that all men desire to see, and having seen once, by even a glimpse, would not give that away for the shows of the rest of the world combined.' Adjacent to and surrounded by Burma, Bangladesh, Tiben and Bhutan, this beautiful 'Queen of the Himalayan Region' is not only one of the biggest tea producers of Inida but is also abundantly renriched with natural beauty.

Scenic Rides

A Ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway

A ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway(DHR) is for those who believe the journey is the destination. The Darjeeling Himalayan Railway or DHR balances on two-feet tracks moving at a maximm speed of 15km an hour. It is a two feet narrow gauge railway. It is a masterpiece of 119th century engineering, a product of the world's first industrial revolution. In 1999, UNESCO declared the DHR a World Heritage Site. The hotel reception can organize tickets for you for a fun filled exciting Joy ride from the Darjeeling station the the Ghoom Railway Station, it is the highest railway station in India and the 2nd highest railway station in the world.

Travel through a gigantic railway loop, where the Toy Train makes a 360 degree turn known as the Batasia Loop. It offers a panoramic view of the Kanchenjunga Range.
