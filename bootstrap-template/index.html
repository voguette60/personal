The Snow Tree

The hotel is situated in the heart of Darjeeling town, 6,000 feet up in the Himalayas. It is a celebration of the Colonial Era in the Queen of Hill Stations, the Snow Tree is very much a part of Darjeeling's colonial history.

Nearly 110 years old and once a summer of the Lord Mountbatten, is now comfortable hotel refurbished and its history kept intact. The rooms offer splendid views of the mountains and valleys and an unforgettable experience that will be long remembered.
